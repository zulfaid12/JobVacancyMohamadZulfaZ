About PT Mzz Industrial Automation

PT Mzz Industrial Automation, founded in 2025 by Mohamad Zulfa Zakariya, is a leading provider of industrial automation solutions in Indonesia. We specialize in designing, implementing, and maintaining automation systems for manufacturing plants across various industries.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians work with cutting-edge technologies including PLC programming, SCADA systems, robotics, and industrial IoT solutions. We pride ourselves on delivering reliable, efficient, and innovative automation solutions that help our clients improve productivity and reduce operational costs.

Headquartered in Blitar, we serve clients throughout Indonesia with a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.
